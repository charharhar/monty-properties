About Us

Corporate Overview

Since 2000, Monty Properties has built a balanced and mature investment portfolio of over 100 high-quality real estate assets spanning across the United States, Hong Kong and China. Our investment-level assets cover the spectrum: commercial properties, residential properties, industrial buildings, and land-based real estate.

As a proud member of the Monty Group, we benefit from over 60 years worth of investment knowledge and trusted connections from across the globe. With an Asian headquarters and international perspective, Monty Properties is advantageously positioned in one of the fastest growing markets in the world.

Our Vision

To maximise sustainable and long-term value to your real estate portfolio through our first-class investment and asset manager.
