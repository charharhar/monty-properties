About Us

Corporate Overview
Since 2000, Monty Properties has built a balanced and mature investment portfolio of over 100 high-quality real estate assets spanning across gateway cities in the United States, Hong Kong and China. Our investment-level assets cover the spectrum: commercial properties, residential properties, industrial buildings and land-based real estate.
As a proud member of the Monty Group. we benefit from over 60 years worth of investment knowledge and trusted connections from across the globe. With an Asian headquarters and international perspective, Monty Properties is advantageously positioned in one of the fastest growing markets in the world.
Our Vision To maximise sustainable and long-term value to your real estate portfolio through our first-class investment and asset manager.
Our Exceptionalism
1 Global Astuteness, Local Expertise Monty Properties has in-depth knowledge of underlying economic foundations and cycles. This insight, along with our in-house real estate professionals and trusted network of local experts, allows us to predict and ensure sustainable, long-term growth.
2 Anticipate Change for Sustainable Growth We know the next decade will not be like the last, and we relish the challenge. Monty Properties prides itself on its acute ability to anticipate change and stay ahead of the curve. Through diverse asset classes and strategic long-term partnerships, we're able to identify and access the best investment opportunities for our clients.
3 Seize and Maximise Value As a member of an established multinational company, Monty Properties can afford to resist quick-fixes and short-term returns. We wait for the ideal moment to invest - and nothing less. Monty Properties inherits the value of close-knit teams and minimal bureaucracy from Monty Group's start in 1947 as a small family business. As such, our asset-focused specialists can proactively identify worthy investments and act decisively for the highest growth potential.
4 Valued, Professional Partnerships With over 60 years of respected investments and operations across a multitude of industries, the Monty Group embraces accountability. This extends to identifying prudent yet creative investment opportunities for our valued partners, as well as ensuring a smooth sales process and offering due diligence on site.
