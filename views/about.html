About Us

Corporate Overview
Since 2000, Monty Property has built a balanced and mature investment portfolio of over 100 high-quality real estate assets spanning gateway cities in the United States, Hong Kong and China. Our investment-level assets cover the spectrum: commercial properties, residential properties, industrial buildings and land-based real estate.
As a proud member of the Monty Group. we benefit from over 60 years worth of investment knowledge and trusted connections from across the globe. With our Asia headquarters and international perspective, Monty Property is advantageously positioned in one of the fastest growing markets in the world.
Our Vision To maximise sustainable and long-term value to our real estate portfolio through first-class investment and asset management practices.
