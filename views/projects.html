Showcase Projects

106 West 56 Street,
New York United States Commercial - Office
The Project is a boutique office building in Manhattan’s most prominent office corridor on 56th Street and developed by one of the best-in-class US developers, Savanna. Learn More

The Project is located in one of the only remaining sites in Midtown Manhattan that can be redeveloped, given most sites in the area are either fully developed or landmarked, which precludes repositioning or demolition. The corridor between 57th to 50th Street, bounded by 6th Avenue and Park Avenue is considered the most prestigious office address, with majority of hedge funds, private equity firms and family offices seeking space in this area.

The Project is expected to complete and operate in Q2 Year 2020.

By investing into a vehicle sponsored by Atom Assets and joint venture with Savanna, this Project demonstrates Monty’s capability to identify and invest high quality assets in global gateway cities such as New York.

Enterprise Square,
Kowloon Hong kong Commercial - Office
The Project is a Grade A office building located in the heart of Kowloon Bay, which is the core district of Kowloon East, another premier CBD of Hong Kong to support its economic growth and strengthen its global competitiveness. Learn More

The Project enjoys excellent proximity to the Kowloon Bay MTR Station and spectacular views of the Victoria Harbour , Cruise Terminal and Zero Carbon Park view.

We acquired one floor in this Project in Year 2006 for both head-quarter use and leasing for investment use, prior to the policy address announced by the Hong Kong Government of its plan to develop Kowloon East as Hong Kong’s second CBD.

By direct acquisition of this Project, it demonstrates Monty’s capability to foresee the changing demographic of the area to enjoy the potential project upside with maturity of the location.

Ben Yuan Building
Shenzhen China Commercial - Office
The Project is a Grade A office building located in the heart of Futian, the CBD of Shenzhen to support its economic growth and strengthen its global competitiveness. Learn More

The Project enjoys excellent proximity to the Shenzhen MTR Station and spectacular city views. Underpinned by the growth of technology sector, Shenzhen has the highest per capita GDP among the four tier-1 cities in Mainland China.

Tertiary industry in Futian has substantially contributed to the district’s GDP, becoming the core commercial hub of Shenzhen. Furthermore, the Government plans to further promote the development of financial services and its CBD connectivity.

We acquired one floor in this Project in Year 2005 for investment purpose, prior to the full maturity of the Futian CBD. It demonstrates Monty’s capability to foresee the changing demographic of the area to enjoy the potential project upside with maturity of the location.
